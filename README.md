# Link-in-Bio

A simple and customizable solution for sharing your most important links in one convenient place. Easily set up a personal landing page (often referred to as a "link-in-bio" page) that you can share on social media platforms (like Instagram, Twitter, or TikTok) to guide your followers to all your content, products, or other online profiles.

## Features

1. **Customizable Layout**  
   - Easily change colors, fonts, and images to fit your personal brand or style.

2. **Responsive Design**  
   - Mobile-friendly layout ensures your links look great on any device.

3. **Lightweight and Fast**  
   - Minimal dependencies and clean design for quick load times.

4. **Analytics (Optional)**  
   - Integrate your favorite tracking service (like Google Analytics) to see how many people visit your link page.

5. **Open Source**  
   - Free to use and modify under an open-source license.
